Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device. Daily Clarity works fully offline and never uploads your entries.

Can I check in more than once a day?
No — Daily Clarity is one intentional check-in per calendar day. Once you've checked in, the app locks until tomorrow.
