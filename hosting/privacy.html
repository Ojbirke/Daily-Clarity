Privacy Policy

Daily Clarity respects your privacy. Our goal is to provide a simple daily check-in without collecting any personal data.

  • No personal data is collected, stored on our servers, or shared with anyone.
  • All your entries stay on your device only.
  • No account and no sign-in are required to use the app.
  • The app works fully offline.

If the app sends you optional daily reminders, those notifications are scheduled locally on your device and are not sent through, or logged by, any server.
